Mango Milkshake

Description

Easy Mango Milkshake my way, with credits to TheWhyteElephant.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 or 1 whole mango
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 - 2 tbsp of condensed milk
  • Optional: whipped cream and slices of the spare mango

Steps:

  1. Add 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream to the blender and blend.
  2. Slice up a mango and add them to the blender with the 1/2 cup of milk.
  3. Blend them all and add any amount of condensed milk for sweetness of your choice.
  4. Get a chilled glass and pour the mixture, and plate it with whipped cream and mango bits. Enjoy!
