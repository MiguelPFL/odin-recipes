Mapo Tofu

Description

Mapo Tofu for four made my way. Had to improvise using other ingredients therefore isn't really authentic in any way.

Ingredients:

  • Tofu for 4 people
  • Ground Beef for 4 people
  • 2 tbsp of Gochujang and 1tbsp of korean soy bean paste
  • Enoki mushrooms or shimeji mushrooms
  • 12 - 16 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 whole diced ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sake/Japaense Rice Wine
  • Corn starch
  • Water
  • Beef stock
  • Red chilies
  • Spring Onion
  • 1 - 4 tbsp chili oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Blanch the cubed up tofu in salted water for 3 minutes. Then drain them and set aside.
  2. Season your ground beef with salt and pepper, while frying the minced garlic, ginger, and mushrooms till there's aroma.
  3. Add in the ground beef along with the sliced red chilies. Once the beef gets color, add the gochujang and soybean paste.
  4. Make a mixture of part water, soy sauce, beef stock, and sake, then add them onto the wok.
  5. Add some chili oil or gochujang if the spice isn't enough. If it is too spicy, add some sugar.
  6. If the mixture is too watery, add a mixture of water and corn starch, and add to the mixture.
  7. Once fully cooked, serve with spring onions, and enjoy!
